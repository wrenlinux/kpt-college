About our College

Kharupetia College is situated at Kharupetia, a fast developing commercial town in Shyamabari Mouza of Mangaldoi Sub-Division under Darrang District. It is about 17 Km. away from Mangaldoi, the District headquarter and 87 Kms from Guwahati, the gateway of NE-region . It is situated by the National Highway No. 15 connecting Baihata Chariali to Mission Chariali, Tezpur.

Message from Principal

Kharupetia College is one of the premier institutions of higher education in Darrang District . Established in the year 1981, the college was taken over by the Govt. of Assam under deficit system of grants-in-aid w.e.f. 25/03/98. The college was provincialized by the Govt. of Assam w.e.f. 01/12/2005 vide Govt. notification No. LPG. 112/2005/158 dated the 19th December, 2005.

Internal Quality Assurance Cell

College has constituted IQAC under the guidelines of National Assurance and Accreditation Council to realize the institutional goals: “Quality Enhancement and Quality Assurance”. The prime objective of the IQAC is to develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the overall performance of the institution. The IQAC works through various Cells and Committee.
